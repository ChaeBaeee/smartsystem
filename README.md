**Smart Study & Academic Progress Monitoring System**


A Kotlin-based system designed to help students manage their studies effectively and enhance academic performance. 


Key Features:

Adaptive Study Schedule – Automatically adjusts to each student’s routine, helping them balance subjects and tasks efficiently.

Study Time Tracking – Logs study duration and helps students stay consistent with their learning goals.

Topic Review Suggestions – Recommends topics to review based on previous performance or study frequency.

Break Reminders – Prevents burnout by reminding students to rest after focused study sessions.

Focus Timer (Customizable Intervals) – Encourages productivity through a flexible time management tool.

Progress Tracking by Subject – Monitors improvement and helps identify weak areas in each subject.

Academic Performance Monitoring – Tracks grades, attendance, and performance trends over time.

Early Alert System – Notifies students and teachers when academic performance drops.

Visual Analytics Dashboards – Displays progress and insights through easy-to-read charts and graphs.
